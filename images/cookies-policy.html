Cookies Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

This Cookies Policy explains how Webburns Tech uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function. They enable core functionality like security and accessibility.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced functionality like remembering preferences.

Marketing Cookies

Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant ads.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics (performance tracking)
  • Facebook Pixel (advertising)
  • Hotjar (user experience analysis)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our cookie usage changes. We will notify you of significant changes.
